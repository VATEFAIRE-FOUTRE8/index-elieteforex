Trading Psychology

The Point-by-Point System

Three mental states that separate legends from amateurs — applied to live Forex trading.

01

Extreme Presence

While a trade is active, it is the most important thing in the world. Total focus on execution and rules.

02

Instant Detachment

Once a trade hits Stop Loss or Take Profit, it is behind you. No emotional residue. The market settled it — move on.

03

The 'Only a Point' Rule

Every trade — win or loss — is just one point in a 1,526-match career. Apply the three reframes:

The Double Fault: A losing trade from a mistake. → Forgive and reset.
The Passing Shot: A loss where you did everything right. → Acknowledge the market's play and move on.
The ESPN Highlight: A massive win. → Don't let ego inflate; it's still just one point in the season.
